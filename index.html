How to Keep Teams Status Green?

If you work from home, you know the struggle of Microsoft Teams or Slack going "Away" (Yellow) after 5 minutes of inactivity. Our Online Mouse Jiggler solves this.

This tool runs a lightweight script in your browser that prevents the computer from entering sleep mode. Because the screen stays awake, your operating system reports "Activity," keeping your status Green (Available).
