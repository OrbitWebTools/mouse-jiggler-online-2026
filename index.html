What is an Online Mouse Jiggler?

An Online Mouse Jiggler is a web-based utility designed to prevent your computer from entering sleep mode or displaying an "Away" status on communication platforms like Microsoft Teams, Slack, Skype, Discord, and Outlook. Unlike physical USB mouse jigglers or downloadable software (.exe files), this tool runs entirely within your web browser.

In the era of remote work (WFH) and hybrid models predicted for 2026, maintaining an "Active" or "Green" status is crucial for employees who want to avoid micromanagement. OrbitWebTools provides a safe, free, and secure solution to keep your workstation awake while you read physical documents, take a break, or perform off-screen tasks.

How Does It Work? (The Technology)

Most people think a mouse jiggler needs to physically move the cursor. That is a myth. To keep a computer awake, you simply need to signal the Operating System (OS) that activity is occurring. OrbitWebTools uses two advanced technologies to achieve this:

  1. HTML5 WakeLock API: This is a modern browser standard that allows a web page to request a "screen keep-awake" lock. It is the same technology used by presentation apps (like Google Slides) to stop your screen from turning off during a presentation.
  2. Invisible Video Loop: As a backup for older browsers, we run a tiny, invisible video loop. Operating Systems are programmed not to sleep while a video is playing (so you can watch Netflix uninterrupted). We leverage this to keep your Teams status green.

Is It Safe? Can IT Detect It?

Safety is the #1 concern for employees. Here is why OrbitWebTools is safer than other methods:

  • No Installation: You do not need admin rights. You are not installing suspicious .exe or .msi files that IT security scanners (like CrowdStrike or SentinelOne) look for.
  • No USB Device: Physical USB jigglers appear as "Unknown USB Devices" in system logs. IT departments often block USB ports to prevent data theft. Our tool uses zero hardware.
  • Browser Sandboxing: This website runs inside the browser's "sandbox". It cannot access your files, your webcam, or your other tabs. It simply tells the browser "Please don't sleep".

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Why is my Teams status still yellow?

Microsoft Teams has a very aggressive idle timer (usually 5 minutes). For this tool to work, you must keep the tab open. You can minimize the browser window, but do not close the tab. Also, ensure your laptop is plugged into power, as some "Battery Saver" modes override browser wake locks.

Does it work on Mac?

Yes! Mac users often use apps like "Caffeine" or "Amphetamine". OrbitWebTools is the best Caffeine alternative for Mac because it requires no installation. It works perfectly on macOS Safari and Chrome.

Does it work on Mobile?

Absolutely. You can use this on iPhone (iOS) and Android devices to keep your screen awake while cooking, reading recipes, or monitoring crypto charts. It prevents the screen auto-lock feature.
